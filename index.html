About Us

Welcome to The Market Pace! Come on over and get great food from one or more of our wonderful kitchens, located in the D Building at Algonquin College. In our neck of the woods you’ll get good food and quick service. We know that our students are very busy and don’t always have a lot of time to sit and eat, so you can either sit in our cafeteria and eat your food on the go in out very portable and microwavable tack way boxes OR have your food delivered to you.

Order Options

We are happy to let you know that we have a new and easy way for YOU to order your food! You can now order your food right from your classroom from your laptop or smartphone. It’s easy. Just click the button and fallow the instructions to tell us exactly what you want to eat. You can either come pick it yourself or have it delivered to you. You is done with your AC Card, so make shore your card has credit.
